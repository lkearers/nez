Jazz Earn

What is Jazz Earn?

Jazz Earn is an app that lets you earn 100 rupees daily!

How to use Jazz Earn?

  1. Download the app using the button below.
  2. Install the app on your device.
  3. Open the app and select whether you want to earn to your Easy Paisa or Jazz Cash account.
  4. Enter your name and account number, and click on submit.
  5. Go to your respective app and claim your rewards for free daily!
